Ingredients

Crafted to a meticulous Japanese standard, every ingredient is carefully selected for purity and purpose.

Considered Ingredients

We leave out propylene glycol, ensuring a cleaner experience. Instead of artificial sugars we opt for the balanced sweetness of monk fruit.

Chef-Crafted Flavor

Each Ki flavor is carefully developed by our Michelin trained chef—balancing depth, contrast, and finish to create a refined sensory experience on the palate.

Long-Lasting Experience

Ki pouches can be enjoyed up to 60 minutes. Our formula delivers a brisk uptake with steady release.

Manufacturing

Every Ki product begins with carefully-sourced ingredients and follows strict quality standards throughout production.

Small-Batch Freshness

Small and frequent production batches ensure you receive the freshest possible product.

Made in the USA

Every Ki product is crafted in US facilities.

Pharmaceutical-Grade

Manufactured under the highest cleanliness standards.

Design

Our eco-conscious design is guided by a philosophy of continuous awareness.

Sustainable Packaging

Our commitment to the planet starts with our environmentally-certified plastic can.

Plant-Based Pouch

Our pouch material uses bamboo viscose for a softer and sustainable experience.

Rooted in Japanese Quality

Embracing the Japanese principle of Kaizen, we continuously improve every step—from sourcing ingredients to our sustainable design. The result is a product that embodies both tradition and progress.
